The National University of Arakan (NUA) was established in 2023 in the historic city of MraukU, known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance. The university was founded with the vision of providing quality education and fostering intellectual growth, research, and community engagement in the region.
Academic Programs:
NUA offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines, including humanities, sciences, social sciences, engineering, and technology. The curriculum is designed to blend traditional knowledge with contemporary skills, preparing students for the challenges of the 21st century.
